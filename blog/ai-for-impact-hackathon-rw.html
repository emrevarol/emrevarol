Guteranya Impano z'Ikoranabuhanga z'Abanyafurika binyuze mu Ihiganwa rya AI for Impact

Ubutumwa Nyakanga 14, 2024

Iki gice kirimo ikiganiro ku mushinga w'ihiganwa rya A2SV — guteza imbere ubuhanga n'ubucuruzi mu miryango y'ikoranabuhanga y'Abanyafurika binyuze mu generative AI.

Ni iki cyateye A2SV gukora ihiganwa, kandi icyerekezo cyari ikihe?

A2SV yahimbye ihiganwa kugira ngo rishyireho ibitekerezo bishobora guhinduka ibicuruzwa "byubatswe n'Abanyafurika, ku bw'Abanyafurika, mu Afurika." Igihe OpenAI yashyiraga ahagaragara interface za generative AI, twamenye ubushobozi bwo guhindura inganda no gutera intege igisekuru gikurikira cy'abayobozi b'ikoranabuhanga b'Abanyafurika. Twifuzaga gukora igikorwa cy'umugabane wose kugira ngo tugaragaze impano z'Abanyafurika no kurenza amahugurwa magufi asanzwe.

Ihiganwa rya 2023 ryagenze rite? Ibisubizo byari ibihe?

Igikorwa cya mbere cyarenze ibyo twari twiteze byose. Nubwo twakoresheje amafaranga make yo kwamamaza, cyageze mu bihugu 47 gifite abitabiriye barenze 3.700. Igisubizo cyari gikomeye, bifite ibitekerezo byiza bivuye mu banyeshuri b'impano bo ku mugabane wose. Twakomeje igiciro cy'ibihembo cya 30.000 $, kandi ihiganwa ryamaze amezi atatu rifite ibyiciro byinshi.

Imiterere y'ihiganwa:

  • Amasegonda: Abitabiriye bafatanyije n'abayobozi b'inganda
  • Igice cya kabiri: Imishinga yashyizweho abajyanama bazi neza, byamaze amezi hafi abiri
  • Ihiganwa rikomeye: Amatsinda 8 akomeye yagiye muri Etiyopiya kugira ngo bagaragaze no gucirwa urubanza

Bamwe mu baje mu ihiganwa rikomeye n'abatsinze bakomeje urugendo rwabo inyuma y'ihiganwa, barishyira mu buryo bwemewe kandi babona inkunga yinyongera.

Ibintu bishya mu byerekeye igice cya 2024 ni ibihe?

Umwaka wa 2024 utangazamakuru y'ubuyobozi bw'isi yose bwagutse, bifite abiyandikishije bava kuri Meta, Google, TikTok, Salesforce, na LinkedIn — bagura cyane ubumenyi bwabo inyuma y'itsinda ry'imbere rya A2SV. Twateje imbere imiterere y'ubufasha kandi twagura urugero rw'igikorwa.

Icyerekezo cy'ejo hazaza cy'ihiganwa ni ikihe?

Imigambi irimo kubaka umuryango w'abashinga bafite abitabiriye bose, sisitemu y'ubufasha yatejwe imbere inyuma y'ihiganwa, no kwagura amaherezo ku mahiganwa ku isi yose, nubwo ubu twibanda ku Afurika. Twifuza gukora urubuga ruhoraho aho ubwenge bwiza buteranyira.

Umurimo wa A2SV wahindutse ute mu myaka 5 ishize?

Mu myaka itanu, A2SV yatanze uburezi bw'ikoranabuhanga bw'ubuntu ku banyeshuri 800+ b'Abanyafurika, yatangije imishinga 7 ifite akamaro yirebana n'ubuzima, uburezi, n'iterambere ry'abaturage, yahaye imirimo 150+ abanyeshuri b'abasoje, kandi yabonye amahirwe arenze 60 y'imirimo mu masosiyete akomeye y'ikoranabuhanga.
