Ubutumwa Mata 15, 2024

Mahlet Dereje Haile: Gukora muri Google na Databricks Byamfashije Gufatanya n'Abantu Bafite Ubwenge Bwinshi

Mahlet Dereje Haile ni umwe mu banyeshuri b'ibanze ba A2SV bagaragaye cyane. Uyu munyamwuga w'ikoranabuhanga yabonye imyitozo muri Google (London) na Databricks (Amsterdam) agiiga muri Kaminuza ya Addis Ababa. Ubu ayobora Akil, umushinga wa A2SV uhuza abakozi b'ubwitange n'imiryango itari iya Leta.

Imiterere n'Intangiriro mu Gukora Porogaramu

Mahlet yamenye gukora porogaramu binyuze mu mushinga wa STEM wa kaminuza hafi y'iwabo, ku birometero 200 uvuye i Addis Ababa. Yitabiriye "Abakobwa 1.000, Ejo Hazaza 1.000," umushinga uhuza abakobwa n'abagore b'abahanga mu bumenyi, kandi yabaye umunyamuryango w'Ishuri Rikuru ry'Ubumenyi rya New York. Urufatiro rwe rukomeye rw'imibare n'ibya fiziki rwamuyoboye ku buhanga bw'ikoranabuhanga.

Kaminuza no Kwinjira muri A2SV

Yize muri Kaminuza ya Addis Ababa, ari umwe mu bagore bake mu mushinga we w'ubuhanga. Igihe umuhuza wa A2SV Emre Varol yasuraga ishami rye, yasabye kandi yinjira mu mwaka we wa gatatu. Yabonye ko porogaramu itanga "gukemura ibibazo nyabyo binyuze mu byiciro by'amakuru na algorithme" inyuma y'amasomo ye ya kaminuza.

Uburambe bwo mu Myitozo

Google (amezi 3)

Nyuma yo gutsinda ibibazo by'umwuga n'ibibazo bibiri kuri telefoni, ibibazo bya viza byatinze ishyirwaho rye muri Amerika. Umwaka ukurikira, yatoye ibibazo bibiri byinyongera kuri telefoni kandi yakira igitekerezo cyo gukora muri Google London.

Databricks (amezi 4)

Yujuje ikibazo cy'umwuga n'ibisuzuma bitatu, amaherezo agaragaza umushinga we muri Databricks Amsterdam.

Ibi birambe byahindure ubuzima — byatumye nfatanya n'abantu bafite ubwenge bwinshi ku mishinga ifite ingaruka ku isi yose.

— Mahlet Dereje Haile

Umurimo w'Ubu: Umushinga Akil

Mahlet ayobora Akil, washyizweho kugira ngo uhuze imiryango itari iya Leta n'abakozi b'ubwitange binyuze mu rubuga rumwe. Umushinga ukemura ikibazo cyo guhuza abakozi b'ubwitange bafite ubumenyi n'imiryango ibakeneye. Ubu uri mu bigeragezo na beta, intambwe zikurikira zirimo gutunganya no gukwirakwiza.

Icyerekezo cy'Ejo Hazaza

Agamije gukoresha uburambe bwe mu nganda z'ikoranabuhanga kugira ngo akemure ibibazo bikomeye by'Abanyafurika, ashyira imbere uruhare rw'ikoranabuhanga mu iterambere ry'abaturage. Mahlet ahagarariye ibyiza A2SV ikora — injeniyeri z'impano zigarurira abaturage babo mu gihe zitsinda ku rwego rw'isi.
